Membership Benefits

Become a member of the Parker Chamber of Commerce. We have been helping local businesses to prosper for 20 years. We work closely with local small or big businesses to offer events, programs, classes, and initiatives to boost your business. We have been working hard with the town Hall of Parker and company to promote Parker city. Parker has become a popular place to live in the last years. Parker is an attractive place for many people. It has many popular events such as the Parker Days, Halloween porch parade, Mayor holiday lights, and much more. These activities are significant opportunities for our members to promote their business. If you want to help us grow Parker city and your business, enjoy Parker’s Chamber of Commerce!

Membership Levels

Parker Chamber of Commerce organization is funded in part by Membership investments. It requires commitment from the business, organizations, and individuals in our community to continue to support our local businesses and City. Parker Chamber of Commerce offers four membership levels. These levels provide a business the opportunity to choose their benefits according to their needs. If you have a question, please call (303)555-3322. We are here to help you and clarify your doubts.

Gold Level

This level includes all benefits of silver, Bronze, and Non-profit Levels.

  • Enhanced 3 category listing.
  • Enhanced Business descriptions of your business. Larger description area, prominent listing. (Ribbon Banner display enabled to make your listing standout.
  • Ability to post photos, videos, and news releases.
  • Members in the News Press Releases (2) submissions/per month. Posts to weekly e-newsletters, website, and social media.
  • One set of chamber member mailing list in electronic format. (no e-mails are provided)
  • Subscription to new residents or new business list.
  • Chamber board Rental 12hrs/year. (max 4/hr increments)
  • Unlimited seats at Business Trainings and Unlimited Passes for Business Unwind Events.)
  • Young Professionals Annual Dues (unlimited employee participation).
  • Annual fee: $1000

Silver Level

This level includes all benefits of Bronze, and Non-profit Levels.

  • Two category listings and a link to your website on the online Directory.
  • Ribbon Cutting/Grand Opening event.
  • Business Unwind/Business Over Breakfast – 12 passes.
  • Annual Leads Group Dues.
  • Use of Chamber’s Bulk Mail Permit.
  • Job Postings on Chamber website.
  • One person may attend trainings.
  • Members in the News/Press Releases (1) submission/per quarter. Posts to weekly enewsletters, website, and social media.
  • Eligibility for a seat on the Chamber Board (After One Year of Membership).
  • Subscription to New Residents OR New Business Lists (1 Time-Quarterly List).
  • Political Advocacy bi-weekly e-newsletter subscription (during legislative session).
  • Annual fee: $400

Bronze Level

This level includes all benefits of the Non-profit Level.

  • Chamber Member Window Cling Decal.
  • One Category Listing, Basic Listing.
  • Business Name, w/link to website (CRC Online Business Directory & Visit Castle Rock Online Directory.
  • Business Cards/Materials displayed in Visitor Center.
  • Certificate of Origin processing.
  • Customer Referrals to your business.
  • Discounted/Reduced Member Pricing.
  • Member to Member Discount Marketing program.
  • Shop The Rock New Residents Program.
  • Weekly Business Insights e-newsletter subscription(Chamber Chatter).
  • Annual fee: $200

Non-Profit Level

  • Inclusion in Member Directory online and magazine
  • Discount advertising Campaign.
  • welcome bags, mailed to new residents quarterly.
  • referrals and leads.
  • direct marketing to members.
  • Office depot discounts.
  • Certify of origin.
  • Leadership classes.
  • Participation in Parker Chambers Events.
  • Subscription to Chamber’s e-newsletters Chamber Chatter and Business Advocacy.
  • Annual fee: $100
