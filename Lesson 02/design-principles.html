White Space

UX Engineer

uxengineer.com

White Space, such as margin, padding, and letter spacing, helps the viewer read or see the page much better. It is crucial to create white space between elements on a web page. A separation between UI elements can help to indicate a relationship or distinction, depending on how much is applied. Good use of white space help to establish hierarchy, promoter rhythm in your design, create balance, improves comprehension, and improve focus on the viewer.

Contrast

Learning Hub

learn.g2.com

Contrast is an essential principle on a website. Every element such as color, font, shape, and pictures play an important role on your page. A web designer must learn how to emphasize essential elements on a web page to get the audience's attention to the page. A good Contrast design only alternates with two or three elements. Using too many elements on a page can confuse or distract viewers from the intended message.
