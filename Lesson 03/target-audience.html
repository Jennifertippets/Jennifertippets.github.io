Target audience

The audience for this web page will be young and adult people. This website will give information about the weather to people who live in Colorado and people who want to visit Colorado. Some people that will visit Colorado will have the correct weather information and a fast and easy way. Today, young people use their phones to find anything. So, it is important to adjust this website to different viewport devices.
